🇱🇺 Luxembourg has been added to the supported countries.
<!--more-->
All holidays according to the [Code du Travail - Art. L. 232-2](https://data.legilux.public.lu/eli/etat/leg/code/travail/20260101) have been added.
Luxembourg is currently available in locales
* Luxembourgish (`lb-LU`)
* Luxembourgish (`de-LU`)
* Luxembourgish (`en-LU`)
* Luxembourgish (`fr-LU`)
